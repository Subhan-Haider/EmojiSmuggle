🕵️ HIDE SECRETS IN PLAIN SIGHT WITH EMOJI STEGANOGRAPHY

Standard encrypted messages raise instant suspicion. Emoji Smuggle changes the game by using advanced Unicode steganography to embed hidden messages, compressed data, and encrypted secrets inside ordinary emojis!

To anyone watching, it’s just a normal string of emojis. To your recipient, it is a highly secure, private channel of communication.

• 100% Invisible Payloads: Uses zero-width Unicode characters to slide secrets between emojis completely invisibly.

• Draggable Floating Action Bubble: Drag the secure overlay bubble anywhere on your screen for real-time encryption and extraction without closing your active chat!
• Smart Clipboard Scanner: Instantly checks for stego payloads in your active clipboard upon app startup.
• Zero Server Footprint: All encoding and decoding happen 100% locally on your device. Complete privacy by design.
• Optional AES-256 Encryption: Lock sensitive data with a password so only people with the key can decode it.

🎮 HOW TO USE:
1. Highlight any text you want to hide in any app.
2. Select "Encode with Emoji Smuggle" from your system text action bar.
3. Share or send the innocent-looking emojis generated!
4. To decode, highlight the emojis, right-click, and select "Decode with Emoji Smuggle".

Perfect for secure note-taking, privacy enthusiasts, and safe communication. Transform innocent emojis into secure carriers today!
